Frequently Asked Questions ??

Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive as a freelance designer. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to reach out—we're happy to help!

1 . What services do you offer?

We specialize in UI/UX design, branding, web design, and development. Whether you need a complete website, a visual identity, or design consultation, we’re here to help bring your ideas to life.

2 . How does the design process work?

Our process begins with discovery and planning, where We understand your goals and audience. Then, We move on to concept creation, revisions based on your feedback, and finalize with testing and delivery. It’s a collaborative process, and We keep you updated every step of the way.

3 . How long does a project usually take?

The timeline depends on the scope of the project. Smaller projects, like logo design, may take 1-2 weeks, while larger projects, such as website design, can take 4-6 weeks. We’ll provide a detailed timeline once we’ve discussed your needs.

4 . What do we need to provide before starting a project?

Typically, We’ll need information about your business, your goals, and any branding assets you already have, like a logo or color palette. For web projects, content like text and images will also be required. Don’t worry—We’ll guide you through this step.

5 . Do you offer revisions?

Yes, revisions are part of Our process to ensure the final design meets your expectations. We usually include 2-3 rounds of revisions depending on the project. Additional revisions can be arranged if needed.

6 . How do I get started?

Simply reach out through my contact page or email us directly. We’ll schedule a consultation to discuss your project, goals, and timeline. From there, We’ll provide a proposal to get things moving.
